Why this exists

A student-led idea rooted in the local community.

Every Chance Forward began with a simple concern: students should not be excluded from learning opportunities because their families cannot afford the same resources as others. The goal is not to make large promises. It is to build something useful, trustworthy, and realistic for Greater Cincinnati—one responsible project at a time.Founder statement · Every Chance Forward
