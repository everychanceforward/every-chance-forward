Every Chance Forward

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026 · Student-led initiative in development

This policy describes the information practices of the Every Chance Forward website and contact form during the initiative’s development stage. Additional notices may apply if future programs launch.

Information we collect

When you voluntarily submit our interest form, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The type of opportunity that interests you
  • Your message

Please do not submit medical, financial, government-identification, or other sensitive information through this form.

How we use information

We use form information only to review your inquiry, respond to you, coordinate volunteer or partnership opportunities, and maintain reasonable records of our communications.

Form processing and service providers

Our form is processed by FormSubmit and delivered to everychanceforward@gmail.com. FormSubmit states that it retains form submissions for up to 30 days so submissions can be recovered if an email is missed or the form has not yet been activated. The emailed copy may remain in our email account until it is no longer reasonably needed.

Our website is hosted through GitHub Pages. These service providers may process limited technical information needed to operate their services under their own policies.

  • FormSubmit documentation
  • GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Google Privacy Policy

Sharing and selling

We do not sell or rent information submitted through the form. We share it only with service providers used to operate the form and email, when necessary to respond to your request, or when disclosure is required by law.

Children’s privacy

The interest form is intended for people age 13 or older, including volunteers, parents or guardians, and community partners. Children under 13 should not submit the form or provide personal information through this website. A parent or guardian who believes a child under 13 submitted information may contact us to request deletion.

Data retention and deletion

We keep form submissions only as long as reasonably necessary to respond, coordinate opportunities, maintain appropriate records, or meet legal obligations. You may request access, correction, or deletion by emailing us. We will make a reasonable effort to honor the request, subject to legal or recordkeeping requirements.

Security

We take reasonable steps to limit access to submitted information. However, no website, email system, or online transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the website or our practices change. The effective date at the top will show when the latest version took effect.

Contact us

For privacy questions or requests, email everychanceforward@gmail.com.
