Our mission

A student’s circumstances should not determine their chances.

Every Chance Forward Foundation exists to help create a community where children can participate in school, build confidence, and move forward regardless of financial hardship.

01

Equal opportunity

We believe every student deserves a fair chance to learn and participate, regardless of household income.

02

Dignity first

Support should respect each student’s privacy, individuality, and ability to make choices.

03

Community centered

Lasting support comes from listening to families and working with trusted schools and local partners.

04

Honest impact

We commit to realistic programs, responsible use of donations, and clear reporting about what we accomplish.
