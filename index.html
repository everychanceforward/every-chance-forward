Our mission

Removing barriers between students and opportunity.

Every Chance Forward Foundation connects under-resourced young people with educational support, essential supplies, trusted mentors, and meaningful experiences.

“A student’s circumstances should never decide how far their potential can take them.”Our guiding belief
01

Practical support

School supplies, books, technology access, clothing, and other essentials that help students participate confidently.

02

Academic growth

Tutoring, study support, and learning resources designed to build skills and confidence.

03

Guidance

Positive mentoring relationships that help young people set goals and navigate new possibilities.

04

Future opportunity

Enrichment, career exploration, and life-skills programs that help students prepare for what comes next.
